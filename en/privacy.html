According to Articles 15-22 of the GDPR, users have the right to request access to their personal data, rectification, erasure, or restriction of processing. 8. Detailed Cookie Information

Cookies are small text strings that websites visited by users send to their terminals, where they are stored to be sent back to the same sites on subsequent visits by the same user.

This website uses the following types of cookies:

  • Technical and Session Cookies: Necessary for the proper functioning of the website, such as remembering your theme preference (dark/light) in localStorage or saving your consent state via the CookieConsent tool. They do not require prior consent.
  • Third-Party (Statistical) Cookies: Google Analytics 4 is used with anonymized IPs and only after your explicit consent expressed through the banner. These cookies allow for the analysis of website usage to improve its features.
